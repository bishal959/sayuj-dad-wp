      About Bajrashali

      We are the oldest Co-operative in Sankhu, helping our community for more than 25 years with simple financial services. We have saving options like personal savings accounts and fixed deposit accounts with good interest rates. We also give loans, such as personal loans and business loans, to help our members reach their money goals. One special service is our remittance, which makes it easy to send and receive money from anywhere in the world. We are a trusted and friendly partner for your finances, and we are happy to serve you!
