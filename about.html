About Us

We are the oldest organization in Sankhu that provides a range of financial services to our members. We offer a variety of saving options, including personal savings accounts and fixed deposit accounts, that provide competitive interest rates. We also provide loan services, including personal loans and business loans, to help our members achieve their financial goals. One of our specialties is the remittance service, which allows our members to easily send and receive money to and from anywhere in the world. We pride ourselves on being a reliable and trustworthy financial partner for our community and we look forward to serving you.
